Marketplace · On-demand services

Hoshi — Home Services Platform

A scalable on-demand marketplace connecting users with trusted technicians — enabling instant service requests, competitive offers, and seamless in-app communication.

Problem

Users struggled to find reliable home service providers with transparent pricing and efficient communication, leading to delays and inconsistent service quality.

Solution

Built a Flutter-based platform enabling users to submit requests, receive multiple technician offers, compare pricing & ratings, and communicate directly inside the app.

Technologies

Flutter Firebase REST APIs Cubit Real-time Chat

Impact

Delivered a smooth, fast user experience that simplifies home service requests, improves transparency, and increases user trust through competitive pricing and ratings.
